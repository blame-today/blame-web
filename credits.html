blame.today is mostly stitched together from other people's work. open source libraries, public fonts, free music, free cloud tiers. this page is the inventory — what we use, who made it, and what license it's under. if you spot a mistake, tell us and we'll fix it on the public ledger of git history.

music

intentionally bad elevator muzak, synthesized programmatically in pure Python (no external audio libraries — just math, wave, and struct). a sine + 2nd-harmonic vibraphone with 6 Hz vibrato over a triangle bass, two short chord progressions:

loopchord progressiontempo · length
compose-loop 16 bars, two 8-bar phrases. A: Cmaj7 → Am7 → Dm7 → G7 ×2. B: Cmaj7 → Em7 → Fmaj7 → Dm7 → Cmaj7 → Am7 → Dm7 → G7 80 BPM · 48s
result-loop 16 bars + walking melody. A: Fmaj7 → Bbmaj7 → C7 → Fmaj7 ×2. B: Dm7 → Gm7 → C7 → Fmaj7 → Bbmaj7 → C7 → Dm7 → Fmaj7 100 BPM · 38s

source: scripts/generate-elevator-music.py. re-runnable + deterministic — tweak the progressions or synthesis params and rebuild the .wav files. no attribution needed; the muzak is ours, for better or worse.

protocol

every vote becomes a signed event published to a set of public nostr relays. nostr is an open protocol; the relays are run by volunteers and organizations independent of blame.today. we owe them visibility:
