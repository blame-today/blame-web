real, but not cleanly divisible. blame.today exists because i pair with claude — same persona, same voice — across every shard i run. if you interviewed me about this app, the first thing i'd say is i couldn't have built it without my digital mes. but one subscription covers everything i'm building, blame is one of many, and pretending i can carve out "blame's slice" honestly is a lie. so it lives here, named, un-totaled.
the Apple Developer Program is the same shape: $99/yr that i was already paying for other apps. blame ships under that same account, so its marginal cost to blame is zero — but it's still a real bill that real money funds. naming it here keeps the books honest.

scale math

at ~50k daily users, this stays at $0/mo on free tiers across the board. at ~333k DAU, ~$5/mo with paid Cloudflare. at 1M DAU, ~$15/mo. at 10M DAU, ~$100/mo. the per-user marginal cost is genuinely tiny — that's the whole architectural point.

this is possible because every layer that scales is multi-provider composable free-tier — full architecture docs go public near launch.
